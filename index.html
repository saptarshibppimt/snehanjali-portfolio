Who I Am

About Me

"

Words are the most inexhaustible source of magic we have.

I am Snehanjali Das — a passionate educator, literary researcher, published author, and Hindustani classical vocalist based in Mumbai.

I believe that language, literature, and music are not separate disciplines — they are all different tongues of the same universal story. My journey has taken me from teaching the basics of grammar to undergraduate students at Mithibai College, to presenting postcolonial critiques at international literary conferences.

In June 2025, I cleared the UGC-NET with a 99.73 percentile on my first attempt, qualifying for both Assistant Professor roles and PhD admission. I also cleared MH-SET in my first attempt, earning eligibility for Assistant Professor posts across Maharashtra and Goa.

Outside the classroom, I am a trained Hindustani classical vocalist, a storyteller who has been published in multiple anthologies, and a lifelong learner who finds joy in every new idea.
